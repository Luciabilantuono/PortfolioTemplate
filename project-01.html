Print / Layout

Editorial Design

A comprehensive print layout project exploring the relationship between typography, imagery, and white space. This editorial piece investigates modern visual hierarchy through careful attention to grid systems and typographic rhythm.

Year 2024
Role Design, Art Direction
Client Studio Project

Overview

This editorial design project explores the delicate balance between form and function in print media. The goal was to create a cohesive visual language that guides readers through complex content while maintaining aesthetic appeal. Through careful consideration of typography, layout, and pacing, the design creates a rhythm that enhances readability and engagement.

Approach

The design process began with extensive research into contemporary editorial design and typographic trends. A modular grid system was developed to provide structure while allowing for creative flexibility. The color palette was kept minimal to emphasize content hierarchy, with strategic use of accent colors to draw attention to key elements.
